About Mountain State Miles

Mountain State Miles is West Virginia's premier source for high school track and cross country results, rankings, and analytics. We're dedicated to providing the running community with accurate, comprehensive data of athletes and teams across the state and providing the running community with accurate, comprehensive data.

Our Mission

To provide West Virginia high school runners and coaches with:

  • Accurate Results: Complete meet results from across the state
  • Rankings: Current rankings for athletes and teams
  • Analytics: Performance trends and insights
  • Community: A hub for the West Virginia running community

What We Offer

📊 Cross Country

Track school rosters, meet results, rankings, and performance analytics for cross country runners statewide.

Track & Field

Complete indoor and outdoor track season data including rankings, records, and athlete profiles.

📈 Analytics

Advanced performance analysis, trends, and predictions to help coaches and athletes track progress.

🏆 Rankings

Real-time rankings updated throughout the season to showcase top performers across the state.

For Coaches & Athletes

Whether you're a coach looking to benchmark your team's performance or an athlete tracking your personal records, Mountain State Miles provides the tools and data you need. Our platform makes it easy to:

  • View detailed meet results and split times
  • Compare performance across the entire season
  • Track progress toward goals
  • See how you stack up against other athletes and teams statewide

Built by Runners, for Runners

Mountain State Miles was created by members of the West Virginia running community who understand the sport and wanted to make it easier for everyone to access and share results. We're committed to continuously improving our platform based on feedback from coaches, athletes, and running enthusiasts.

Data Sources

Our data comes from mostly athletic.net for the time being. If you'd like to submit results or have a suggestion for improvement, reach out to us through our GitHub repository (https://github.com/sjwooft1/mountainstatemiles).
